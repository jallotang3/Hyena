《定风波·行侠令》视频提示词
=====================================

【整体风格基调】

Overall style: ancient Chinese wuxia aesthetic, ink wash painting (水墨风格),
cinematic wide shots, dramatic lighting, slow motion with dynamic cuts,
color palette: deep indigo, charcoal black, silver moonlight, amber torch light,
dusty golden desert tones. 4K, film grain texture.


=====================================
【引子】剑挑寒星出鞘时 / 江湖万里起长风
=====================================

A lone swordsman standing on a mountain cliff at dawn,
slowly drawing a ancient Chinese sword from its scabbard.
The blade catches starlight and moonlight simultaneously.
Wind rushes across the mountain, scattering cherry blossom petals.
Ultra-wide cinematic shot, low angle looking up at the figure silhouetted
against a vast ink-blue sky filled with stars.
Slow motion sword draw, dramatic orchestral buildup.
Chinese ink painting style with realistic rendering.


=====================================
【第一段】莫问此行路几重 / 一声长啸破苍穹 / 踏碎山河无所惧 / 烟雨茫茫 任飘蓬
=====================================

Scene 1: A solitary swordsman walking along an ancient mountain road
shrouded in mist, wearing a gray travel cloak, sword on back.
Shot from behind, expansive mountains fading into clouds ahead.

Scene 2: The swordsman stops, throws his head back and lets out a
fierce cry toward the sky. Camera rotates 360° around him.
Eagles scatter from cliffs. Dramatic sky with rolling clouds.

Scene 3: Rapid montage — boots stepping through river shallows,
crossing rope bridges over gorges, climbing icy stone steps.
Dynamic tracking shot.

Scene 4: Heavy rain and fog. The swordsman walks calmly through
the downpour without hurrying, cloak trailing in the wind.
Slow motion rain drops catching torchlight. Melancholic beauty.


=====================================
【导歌】功名如梦随云散 / 天地之大 任我行
=====================================

Aerial drone shot pulling back from the swordsman
to reveal an impossibly vast landscape —
ancient rivers, endless mountain ranges,
lone figure becoming a tiny silhouette.
Ink-wash clouds dissolve and reform like fading dreams.
Golden hour light bathes the mountains.
Time-lapse sky movement, slow camera ascent.


=====================================
【副歌】谁怕 谁怕 / 长风破浪赤子心 / 万里江湖皆是故乡 / 来去从容
=====================================

High-energy sequence:

Shot 1: Swordsman leaps from a cliff,
silhouetted against the sun, slow-motion freefall into mist below.

Shot 2: Running across rooftops of an ancient Chinese town at dusk,
lanterns swaying, crowd below unaware.

Shot 3: Close-up face — eyes calm, slight smile,
wind blowing hair dramatically. Extreme confidence.

Shot 4: Wide shot — swordsman walks away from camera
into a vast open plain, arms slightly spread,
as if embracing the whole world.
Camera slowly rises. Epic scale.


=====================================
【第二段】醉卧黄沙听角声 / 梦里刀光映古城 / 半壶烈酒半生路 / 恩怨情仇 付苍冥
=====================================

Scene 1: Desert at twilight. The swordsman lies on golden sand dunes,
arm behind head, eyes closed.
A distant horn echoes. Embers of a dying campfire nearby.

Scene 2: Dream sequence — ethereal, desaturated.
Ancient walled city burning in the distance.
Sword flashes reflected in a puddle of rainwater.
Slow, ghostly movement.

Scene 3: Close-up hands wrapping around a rough ceramic wine jug.
Firelight. Weathered scars on knuckles.
Slow zoom out to reveal the solitary figure by the fire,
vast starry desert sky above.

Scene 4: Symbolic shot — the swordsman opens his palm,
a folded letter or token slowly lifted by wind and carried
into the night sky, dissolving into stars.
Letting go of the past.


=====================================
【Bridge】回首来时风与雪 / 是非对错已云烟 / 不问前路有多远 / 放下执念 天地宽
=====================================

Scene 1: The swordsman turns to look back —
behind him, a long mountain road disappearing into blizzard snow.
Melancholic, reflective expression. Warm amber vs cold blue contrast.

Scene 2: Time-lapse montage — seasons changing over the same mountain path.
Snow melts, flowers bloom, leaves fall, snow returns.
The swordsman ages subtly through each season.

Scene 3: The swordsman unsheathes his sword slowly,
then gently sets it down on a stone altar by a mountain stream.
He straightens up. His shoulders visibly relax.
Close-up on the sword resting alone.
Camera slowly pulls away.

Scene 4: Wide shot — infinite open sky after a storm has passed.
Rays of light breaking through parting clouds.
The swordsman standing still, arms at sides, breathing deeply.


=====================================
【尾声】归去—— / 山河无恙 / 也无风
=====================================

Final sequence — 3 shots, slow dissolves between each:

Shot 1: The swordsman walks away from camera
into a mist-covered valley at golden hour.
His figure slowly fades into the morning haze.
No music, only wind.

Shot 2: Aerial wide shot —
ancient China landscape in perfect stillness.
Rivers, mountains, fields. No person visible.
Just the world, serene and eternal.

Shot 3: Extreme close-up —
a single cherry blossom petal
floating gently down to rest
on the still surface of a mountain lake.
Ripples spread, then fade.
Fade to black.
